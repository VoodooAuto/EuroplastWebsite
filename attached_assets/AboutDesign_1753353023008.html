About Europlase Ltd.

Our Story

Founded in 1998, Europlase Ltd. began as a small operation focused on providing high-quality bulk bags and woven PP sacks to local businesses. Over the years, we have grown into a leading manufacturer, serving a diverse range of industries across Europe. Our commitment to innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success.

Mission Statement

To deliver superior packaging solutions that meet the evolving needs of our customers while upholding the highest standards of quality, reliability, and environmental responsibility.

Our Values
